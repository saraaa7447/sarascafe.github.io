Lenovo ThinkPad X61

CPU: Intel Core 2 Duo T7100 1.8 GHz

Memory: 2 GB

Storage: 80 GB SATA HDD

GPU: Intel Graphics Media Accelerator (GMA) X3100 128 MB VRAM

Condition: Working perfectly

I got this machine recently as I've always wanted to own a non-Tablet X-series laptop. Compared to them it's quite a bit smaller and is probably my second or third smallest machine. I absolutely love the form factor of it. Keyboard feels amazing and the lack of a touchpad is a plus for me since I never use it. It has the bigger battery and it lasts around 2 hours. Mine is lacking the fingerprint reader though. It currently has Void Linux installed and runs quite well.
